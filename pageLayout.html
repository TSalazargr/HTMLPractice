Article tag

article represents an independent, self-contained piece of content. An article is content that would make sense on its own.

  • article represents a self-contained, independent piece of content.
  • section is used to divide content into parts
  • aside is used for secondary, additional or somehow related content

Section tag

section is a semantic tag. It usually includes a heading. section can be used to separate each chapter, news item, author bio, etc.

Aside tag

The aside HTML element represents a portion of a document whose content is only indirectly related to the document's main content. It's used for secondary, additional or somehow related content.
